Iron Man

Tony Stark

Genius. Billionaire. Philanthropist

Origin

Tony Stark built his first Iron Man suit when he was a prisoner of a terrorist warlord. He was inspired to build a full body suit made of Iron with the help of a fellow prisoner. Together they created the very first Iron Man suit and Tony Stark was the only survivor when escaping. Since then, Tony has been improving his armour, integrating his AI to the suit, and eventually using nano-tech
